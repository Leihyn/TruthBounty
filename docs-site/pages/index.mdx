# TruthBounty Documentation

<Callout type="info" emoji="ğŸ¯">
  **Decentralized Reputation Protocol for Prediction Markets**

  TruthBounty aggregates prediction market performance across multiple platforms into a unified, soulbound NFT-based identity.
</Callout>

## What is TruthBounty?

TruthBounty solves **reputation fragmentation** in prediction markets. Traders build track records across multiple platforms (PancakeSwap, Polymarket, Azuro, etc.), but this history is siloed and non-portable.

### The Solution

## Quick Start

<Steps>

### Connect Your Wallet

Visit [TruthBounty](https://truth-bounty-4r9b.vercel.app) and connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)

### Register & Mint NFT

Pay a small fee (0.0005 BNB) to mint your soulbound Reputation NFT

### Import Predictions

Connect platforms and import your prediction history to build your TruthScore

### Climb the Leaderboard

Compete globally and enable copy trading when you reach Gold tier

</Steps>

    - **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
