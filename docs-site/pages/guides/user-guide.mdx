# User Guide

This guide explains how to use TruthBounty as a prediction market trader or copy trader.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
- BNB for gas fees (testnet or mainnet)
- Prediction market activity on supported platforms

### Connecting Your Wallet

<Steps>

### Visit TruthBounty

Go to [TruthBounty](https://truthbounty.xyz)

### Connect Wallet

Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top right

### Select Provider

Choose your wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)

### Approve Connection

Approve the connection request in your wallet

### Verify Network

Ensure you're on BNB Chain (Mainnet or Testnet)

</Steps>

## Registration

### Minting Your Reputation NFT

<Callout type="info">
  Your TruthBounty identity is a **soulbound NFT** - a non-transferable token that represents your prediction market reputation.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Navigate to Dashboard

Click on "Dashboard" in the navigation

### Click Register

Click the **"Register"** button

### Confirm Transaction

Approve the transaction in your wallet (0.0005 BNB fee)

### Wait for Confirmation

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed

### NFT Minted

Your soulbound NFT is now minted and tied to your wallet

</Steps>

**What You Get:**
- Unique soulbound NFT tied to your wallet
- Starting TruthScore of 0
- Bronze tier status
- Access to import predictions

## Copy Trading

### Overview

<Callout type="info">
  Copy trading allows you to automatically mirror trades from top performers.
</Callout>

### Becoming a Follower

**Requirements:**
- Registered with NFT
- BNB in your wallet

<Steps>

### Go to Copy Trading

Navigate to **Copy Trading** page

### Browse Leaders

Browse available leaders (Gold tier and above)

### Click Follow

Click **"Follow"** on a trader you want to copy

### Configure Settings

Set your preferences:
- **Allocation %**: How much of leader's bet to copy (e.g., 50%)
- **Max Bet**: Maximum per trade (e.g., 0.5 BNB)

### Deposit to Vault

Deposit BNB to your copy trading vault

### Confirm Follow

Confirm the follow transaction

</Steps>

### Fee Structure

| Fee Type | Amount | When Charged |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Protocol Fee | 10% | On profits only |
| Leader Fee | 10% | On profits only |

**Example:**
```
You copy a trade that wins 0.1 BNB profit:
- Protocol takes: 0.01 BNB (10%)
- Leader takes: 0.01 BNB (10%)
- You keep: 0.08 BNB (80%)
```

### Simulation Mode

<Callout>
  Test copy trading without real funds using Simulation Mode.
</Callout>

1. Enable **"Simulation Mode"** toggle
2. Follow traders normally
3. Track virtual P&L
4. Switch to real mode when ready

## Becoming a Leader

### Requirements

- Gold tier or higher (TruthScore 500+)
- Minimum 50 predictions
- Account age > 14 days

### Benefits

- 10% of follower profits
- Increased visibility on leaderboard
- Premium badge on profile

### Enabling Copy Trading

<Steps>

### Reach Gold Tier

Build your TruthScore to 500+ (Gold tier)

### Open Settings

Go to **Profile** > **Settings**

### Enable Copy Trading

Toggle **"Allow Copy Trading"**

### Confirm

Confirm the transaction

</Steps>

## Tips for Success

### Building Your Score

1. **Start Small**: Build consistency before volume
2. **Diversify**: Trade across multiple platforms
3. **Be Patient**: 50+ bets needed for full score
4. **Track Performance**: Use analytics to improve

### Copy Trading Tips

1. **Research Leaders**: Check their full history
2. **Start with Simulation**: Test before real funds
3. **Diversify**: Follow multiple traders
4. **Set Limits**: Use max bet settings
5. **Monitor**: Check performance regularly

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
