Appendix B: TruthScore Formula

TruthScore = (winRate × volumeSqrt) / 100

where:
  winRate = (correctPredictions / totalPredictions) × 10000
  volumeSqrt = √(totalVolume / 1e16)

Example Calculation:

  • 100 predictions, 70 correct
  • 50 BNB total volume
  • winRate = (70/100) × 10000 = 7000
  • volumeSqrt = √(50 × 10^18 / 10^16) = √5000 ≈ 70.71
  • TruthScore = (7000 × 70.71) / 100 = 4,950 (Platinum)
