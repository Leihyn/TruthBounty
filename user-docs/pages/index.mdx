## Why Your Reputation Matters

In prediction markets, your track record is everything. But right now:

- Your history is scattered across different platforms
- There's no way to prove your skills
- New traders can't find trustworthy people to follow

TruthBounty fixes this by creating a **unified reputation system** that:

- Aggregates all your predictions in one place
- Provides verifiable, on-chain proof of your skills
- Lets successful traders monetize their expertise
