## Using Simulation Effectively

### Test Multiple Leaders

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-blue-50 dark:bg-blue-900/20 mt-4">
  **Strategy:** Split your virtual funds across several leaders to compare:
  - Leader A: 30% of virtual funds
  - Leader B: 30% of virtual funds
  - Leader C: 40% of virtual funds

  After a few weeks, you'll see who performs best.
</div>

### Track Over Time

<Callout>
  Short-term simulation isn't meaningful. Run simulations for at least 2-4 weeks to get reliable data.
</Callout>

### Document Your Observations

Keep notes on:
- Which leaders are most consistent?
- How do they handle losing streaks?
- What's the typical drawdown?
- Are they active enough?

## Transitioning to Real Trading

Ready to go live? Here's how:

<Steps>

### Review simulation results

Ensure you're happy with the leader's performance

### Start small

Begin with a smaller amount than you eventually want to invest

### Use the same leaders

Follow the leaders who performed well in simulation

### Monitor closely at first

Pay more attention during your first few real trades

### Scale up gradually

Increase your position as you gain confidence

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  **Important:** Past simulation performance doesn't guarantee future real results. Markets can change, and real trading has additional factors (gas, slippage, timing).
</Callout>

## Limitations of Simulation

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-4">
  **Be aware of these differences:**

  | Simulation | Reality |
  |------------|---------|
  | No slippage | Trades may have slippage |
  | Perfect timing | Slight delays possible |
  | No gas costs | Gas reduces net gains |
  | Psychological ease | Real money = emotions |
</div>

### The Psychology Factor

Simulation is low-stress. Real trading isn't:
- Losses feel more painful
- You might panic sell
- FOMO affects decisions
- Confidence can waver

Prepare yourself for the emotional difference.

## Simulation Tips

### Do's

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-green-50 dark:bg-green-900/20 mt-4">
  <ul className="space-y-2 mb-0">
    <li>**Run for several weeks** - Short tests are meaningless</li>
    <li>**Try multiple leaders** - Compare side by side</li>
    <li>**Track everything** - Notes help you remember insights</li>
    <li>**Be honest** - Would you really have held through that drawdown?</li>
    <li>**Simulate your actual budget** - If you have 5 BNB, simulate with 5 BNB</li>
  </ul>
</div>

### Don'ts

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-red-50 dark:bg-red-900/20 mt-4">
  <ul className="space-y-2 mb-0">
    <li>**Rush to real trading** - Patience pays</li>
    <li>**Ignore losses** - Learn from what doesn't work</li>
    <li>**Overfit** - One good week doesn't mean much</li>
    <li>**Skip the emotional prep** - Real money feels different</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## FAQ

**Do I need to connect my wallet for simulation?**
Yes, for tracking and to access the feature, but no real transactions occur.

**Can I simulate as a leader?**
Simulation is currently for followers evaluating leaders.

**Is there a time limit on simulation?**
No! Simulate as long as you need.

**Do simulation results affect my TruthScore?**
No. Simulation is completely separate from your real activity.

**Can I simulate multiple leaders at once?**
Yes! That's the best way to compare.
