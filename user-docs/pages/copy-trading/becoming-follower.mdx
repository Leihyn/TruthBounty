## Getting Started

<Steps>

### Check Requirements

**You need:**
- ‚úÖ Any tier (even Bronze)
- ‚úÖ Connected wallet
- ‚úÖ Funds to deposit (BNB for BNB Chain vaults)

<Callout type="info">
  No minimum TruthScore required to follow! This is accessible to everyone.
</Callout>

### Find Leaders to Follow

Go to **Copy Trading ‚Üí Find Leaders** to browse available vaults.

**What to look for:**

| Factor | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| **TruthScore** | Higher = more proven track record |
| **Win Rate** | Accuracy of predictions |
| **Volume** | Experience level |
| **Platforms** | What they trade (PancakeSwap, Polymarket, etc.) |
| **Active Days** | How long they've been trading |
| **Followers** | Social proof (but don't follow blindly) |

### Review Their History

Click on a leader to see detailed stats:

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-gray-50 dark:bg-gray-800 mt-2">
  **Check these carefully:**
  - Recent performance (not just all-time)
  - Win/loss distribution
  - Average bet sizes
  - Trading frequency
  - Worst drawdowns
</div>

### Deposit into Their Vault

1. Click "Follow" on the leader's profile
2. Enter deposit amount
3. Confirm transaction
4. Done! You're now copying their trades.

### Monitor Performance

Track your copy trading position from **Dashboard ‚Üí Copy Trading**.

</Steps>

## How Copying Works

### The Process

```
1. Leader decides to bet "BNB UP" with 1 BNB
2. Smart contract detects the trade
3. Your vault allocation copies proportionally
4. If leader wins, profits distributed to all followers
5. Fees collected on profits only
```

### Proportional Allocation

Your copy size depends on your deposit relative to the vault:

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-blue-50 dark:bg-blue-900/20 mt-4">
  **Example:**
  - Total vault: 100 BNB
  - Your deposit: 5 BNB (5% of vault)
  - Leader bets: 10 BNB
  - Your copy: 0.5 BNB (5% of leader's bet)
</div>

## Managing Your Position

### Viewing Status

From your Dashboard ‚Üí Copy Trading:
- Current balance
- Profit/loss
- Pending trades
- Recent copies

### Withdrawing

You can withdraw from a vault anytime:

1. Go to the vault page
2. Click "Withdraw"
3. Choose amount (full or partial)
4. Confirm transaction
5. Funds return to your wallet

<Callout type="warning">
  **Pending trades:** If there are unresolved predictions, that portion may be locked until they settle.
</Callout>

### Switching Leaders

Not happy with a leader's performance?

1. Withdraw from current vault
2. Find a new leader
3. Deposit into their vault

There's no penalty for switching!

## Troubleshooting

### "Deposit failed"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Possible causes:**
  - Insufficient funds
  - Not enough gas
  - Below vault minimum

  **Solution:** Check balance, add gas, or try a larger deposit
</div>

### "Withdraw pending"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Cause:** You have funds in pending trades that haven't resolved

  **Solution:** Wait for trades to settle, then withdraw
</div>

### "No copies happening"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Cause:** Leader hasn't traded recently

  **Solution:** This is normal during quiet periods. Check if the leader is still active.
</div>

## FAQ

**What happens if a leader stops trading?**
You can withdraw anytime. Consider finding a new leader.

**Can a leader see my identity?**
No. Leaders see aggregate vault stats, not individual followers.

**Is there a maximum I can deposit?**
Individual vaults may have caps. Check the vault details.

**What if the leader's strategy changes?**
Monitor regularly. If their approach shifts, reassess whether to continue following.
