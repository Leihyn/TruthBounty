## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Why 20% total fees?**
It's an industry-standard split that fairly compensates both the platform (for development and maintenance) and leaders (for their expertise).

**Can fees change?**
Fee structure is set in smart contracts. Any changes would require significant protocol updates.

**Are fees negotiable?**
No. Fees are the same for everyone to ensure fairness.

### For Followers

**Do I pay fees if the leader loses?**
No! Fees are only on profits.

**What if I withdraw before trade settles?**
You can't withdraw funds locked in pending trades. Wait for settlement.

**Do I pay fees when withdrawing?**
No TruthBounty withdrawal fee. Just network gas.

### For Leaders

**How often can I claim fees?**
Anytime! Daily, weekly, monthly - your choice.

**What if I have no profit trades?**
No profits = no fees to claim.

**Are fees cumulative?**
Yes. Unclaimed fees accumulate until you claim them.
