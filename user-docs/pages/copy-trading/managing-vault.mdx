<Steps>

### Full Withdrawal

To exit a vault completely:

1. Click "Withdraw All"
2. Confirm transaction
3. All available funds return to wallet
4. You stop copying that leader

### Pausing Your Vault

Need a break? Pause your vault:

1. Go to Vault Settings
2. Toggle "Pause Trading"
3. New trades won't be copied
4. Existing positions remain active

**When to pause:**
- Taking time off
- Market conditions too volatile
- Testing new strategy
- Personal circumstances

### Resuming Trading

Simply toggle off the pause:
1. Go to Vault Settings
2. Toggle off "Pause Trading"
3. Future trades copy again

## Troubleshooting

### "Withdraw unavailable"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Cause:** Funds locked in pending trades

  **Solution:** Wait for trades to resolve, then try again
</div>

### "Fee claim failed"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">

  **Solution:** Check fee balance and gas in wallet
</div>

### "Balance doesn't match expected"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Cause:** Pending trades or fees not yet calculated

  **Solution:** Wait for pending items to resolve; balance updates accordingly
</div>

## FAQ

**How often should I check my position?**
For followers: Weekly is usually enough unless actively trading. For leaders: Daily or as often as you trade.

**Can I add to a vault while trades are pending?**
Yes! New deposits are separate from existing positions.

**Do I lose fees if followers withdraw?**
No. Your fee earnings are separate from follower balances.

**What happens if gas prices spike?**
You may need more ETH/BNB for transactions, but the vault logic is unaffected.
