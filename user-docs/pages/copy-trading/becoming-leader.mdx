## Setting Up Your Vault

<Steps>

### Verify Tier Status

Go to your Dashboard and confirm you're Gold tier or above.

### Navigate to Copy Trading

Click **Copy Trading ‚Üí Create Vault**

### Configure Your Vault

Set up your vault parameters:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Vault Name** | Display name for your vault |
| **Description** | Explain your strategy (optional) |
| **Minimum Deposit** | Lowest amount followers can deposit |
| **Platforms** | Which platforms you'll trade on |

### Deploy Contract

Click "Create Vault" and confirm the transaction. This deploys your personal vault contract.

### Start Trading

Trade as normal on supported platforms. Your followers will automatically copy your trades!

</Steps>

## Leader Responsibilities

### What's Expected

As a leader, you're responsible for:

1. **Trading decisions** - Your calls become everyone's calls
2. **Risk management** - Followers trust you with their capital
3. **Consistency** - Regular activity keeps followers confident
4. **Honesty** - Your stats are public; don't try to game them

### What You're NOT Responsible For

<Callout>
  You are NOT responsible for:
  - Guaranteeing returns

  - Market conditions
  - Platform issues

## FAQ

**Can I have multiple vaults?**
Currently, one vault per wallet.

**What if I drop below Gold tier?**
Your vault remains active, but new followers may be hesitant.

**Can I close my vault?**
Yes, but followers must withdraw first. You can pause it instead.

**Do I have to trade differently?**
No! Trade as normal. The system copies automatically.

**Can followers see my trading strategy?**
They can see your trades and stats, but not any off-chain analysis you do.
