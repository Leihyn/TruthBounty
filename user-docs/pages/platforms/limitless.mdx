## How Limitless Works

<Steps>

### Connect to Base

Make sure your wallet is connected to Base chain.

### Browse markets

Find markets you have opinions on. Categories include:
- Crypto events
- Sports outcomes
- Entertainment
- Politics
- Random/fun predictions

### Check the odds

Like Polymarket, prices reflect implied probability:
- $0.30 = 30% implied chance
- $0.70 = 70% implied chance

### Buy shares

- Buy "Yes" if you think it will happen
- Buy "No" if you think it won't

### Wait for resolution

When the event resolves:
- Winning shares pay $1.00
- Losing shares pay $0.00

</Steps>

## FAQ

**Is Limitless related to Polymarket?**
No, they're separate platforms. Limitless operates on Base, Polymarket on Polygon.

**Can I create my own markets?**
Limitless allows community market proposals. Check their docs for details.

**What about liquidity?**
Limitless is growing. Popular markets have good liquidity, niche ones may be thinner.

**How does resolution work?**
Markets are resolved by designated resolvers or oracles, depending on the market type.
