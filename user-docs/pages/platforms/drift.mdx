## How Drift BET Works

<Steps>

### Connect Solana wallet

Use Phantom, Solflare, or other Solana wallets.

### Fund with USDC

Deposit USDC to your Drift account.

### Select a market

Browse available prediction markets on Drift.

### Take a position

Buy YES or NO shares based on your prediction.

### Resolution

Markets resolve based on oracle data or governance.

</Steps>

## Importing to TruthBounty

<Callout type="info">
  Drift trades are on Solana. TruthBounty reads your prediction history using your Solana wallet address.
</Callout>

### What Gets Imported

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Market** | Event being predicted |
| **Position** | YES or NO |
| **Amount** | USDC staked |
| **Result** | Win or Loss |
| **Payout** | Amount received |

<Callout type="warning">
  Make sure you're using the same Solana address you trade with on Drift.
</Callout>
