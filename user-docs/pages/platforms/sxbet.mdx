## How SX Bet Works

<Steps>

### Connect to SX Network

Add the SX Network to your wallet and bridge funds.

### Browse markets

Find sports or esports events you want to bet on.

### Place your bet

Choose your selection and stake amount in USDC.

### Wait for settlement

When the event ends, your bet is automatically settled.

</Steps>
