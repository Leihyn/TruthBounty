## How Azuro Works

<Steps>

### Find an event

Browse upcoming sports events on any Azuro-powered frontend (like bookmaker.xyz or others).

### Choose your bet

Select from various bet types:
- **Moneyline** - Who will win?
- **Spread/Handicap** - Win by how much?
- **Over/Under** - Total points/goals

### Place bet on-chain

Your bet is recorded on the blockchain. You receive an NFT representing your position.

### Wait for event

When the game ends, Azuro's oracles report the result.

### Automatic settlement

Winners receive payouts automatically. No need to claim manually.

</Steps>

## Tips for Sports Betting

### General Strategy

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-green-50 dark:bg-green-900/20 mt-4">
  <ul className="space-y-2 mb-0">
    <li>**Specialize** - Focus on 1-2 sports/leagues you know well</li>
    <li>**Line shopping** - Compare odds across platforms</li>
    <li>**Injury news** - Check team news before betting</li>
    <li>**Weather** - Affects outdoor sports significantly</li>
    <li>**Rest days** - Teams on back-to-backs often underperform</li>
    <li>**Historical data** - Head-to-head records matter</li>
  </ul>
</div>

### Common Mistakes

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-red-50 dark:bg-red-900/20 mt-4">
  <ul className="space-y-2 mb-0">
    <li>**Fan bias** - Betting on your favorite team regardless of odds</li>
    <li>**Chasing favorites** - Heavy favorites often don't cover</li>
    <li>**Parlays** - Multiple-leg bets are hard to win</li>
    <li>**Recency bias** - Overweighting last game's performance</li>
    <li>**Ignoring value** - Chasing popular picks, not good odds</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## FAQ

**Does Azuro have parlays?**
Yes! Azuro supports combo bets. Each leg of a parlay counts as a separate prediction for TruthBounty purposes.

**What about live betting?**
Live (in-play) bets are supported. They count the same as pre-match bets.

**How are pushed bets handled?**
If a bet is voided (cancelled), it doesn't count toward your stats.

**Which frontend do you track?**
It doesn't matter which Azuro frontend you use (bookmaker.xyz, etc.) - all bets go to the same contracts.
