## How Overtime Works

<Steps>

### Connect wallet to Optimism

Overtime runs on Optimism L2. You'll need ETH for gas and sUSD or USDC for betting.

### Browse upcoming games

Find sports events you want to bet on. Overtime shows odds and available markets.

### Choose your market

Pick from:
- **Moneyline** - Pick the winner
- **Spread** - Pick winner with handicap
- **Total** - Over/Under points

### Place bet

Specify amount and confirm. Your position is minted as an NFT.

### Automatic settlement

After the game ends, winning positions can be claimed. Overtime uses oracles for results.

</Steps>

The favorite "gives" points to the underdog:

  - Chiefs -7.5: Bet wins if Chiefs win by 8+

## Troubleshooting

### "Can't find my bets"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Possible causes:**
  - Not on Optimism network
  - Only have open positions (games not finished)
  - Used a different wallet

  **Solution:** Verify wallet address and switch to Optimism
</div>

### "Old bets missing"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Cause:** Very old bets from legacy contracts may not import

  **Solution:** Most recent Overtime activity should import fine. Legacy V1 bets may require manual support.
</div>

### "Wrong odds showing"

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-2">
  **Cause:** We show the odds at the time you placed the bet, which may differ from final line

  **Solution:** This is expected behavior - your bet locked in at your entry odds
</div>

## FAQ

**Does Overtime work in the US?**
Overtime is accessible globally. Legal compliance is the user's responsibility.

**What about sUSD vs USDC?**
Both work. We convert to USD equivalent for TruthScore calculations.

**Can I bet live (in-play)?**
Overtime recently added live betting. These bets are imported like regular bets.

**How fast do results settle?**
Usually within an hour of game end. Oracles verify results before settlement.
