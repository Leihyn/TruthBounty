## How PancakeSwap Prediction Works

<Steps>

### A round starts

Every 5 minutes, a new prediction round begins. You see the current BNB price as the "lock price."

### You place your bet

Before the round locks, you choose:
- **UP (Bull)** - You think BNB will go higher
- **DOWN (Bear)** - You think BNB will go lower

### Round locks

No more bets accepted. The price at this moment becomes the "lock price."

### Round ends

After 5 minutes, the final price is compared to the lock price:
- Price went UP ‚Üí Bulls win
- Price went DOWN ‚Üí Bears win

### Payouts

Winners split the losing pool (minus a small fee). Better odds = bigger payout!

</Steps>

## Understanding Your Stats

### Win Rate

Your PancakeSwap win rate is calculated as:

```
Win Rate = (Winning Rounds / Total Rounds) √ó 100
```

<Callout>
  A 55%+ win rate is considered good on PancakeSwap. The best traders hover around 60-70%.
</Callout>

### Volume

Your total BNB wagered across all rounds. This contributes to your TruthScore.

### ROI (Return on Investment)

```
ROI = ((Total Winnings - Total Wagered) / Total Wagered) √ó 100
```

Positive ROI means you're profitable!

## FAQ

**How far back can TruthBounty import?**
We can import your entire history from the beginning of PancakeSwap Prediction.

**Do cancelled rounds count?**
No. Only completed rounds with a clear winner are counted.

**What about the house edge?**
We calculate your win rate based on correct predictions, not profitability. The house edge affects payouts but not your prediction accuracy.

**Can I import from the CAKE prediction pool?**
Currently, we only support BNB predictions. CAKE may be added in the future.
