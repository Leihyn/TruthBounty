## How Kalshi Works

<Steps>

### Create an account

Sign up and complete KYC verification (US residents only).

### Fund your account

Deposit USD via bank transfer or wire.

### Browse markets

Find event contracts that interest you.

### Buy contracts

Purchase YES or NO contracts at market prices.

### Settlement

Contracts pay out $1 if correct, $0 if incorrect.

</Steps>

### What Gets Imported

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Event** | Market question |

| **Amount** | USD wagered |
| **Price** | Price paid per contract |
| **Result** | Win or Loss |
| **Payout** | Amount received |
