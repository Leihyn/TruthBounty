## How Manifold Works

<Steps>

### Create a free account

Sign up with Google or email - no payment required.

### Get free Mana

New users receive starting Mana to begin trading.

### Browse or create markets

Trade on existing markets or create your own.

### Trade with Mana

Buy and sell shares to express your predictions.

### Earn more Mana

Accurate predictions earn you more Mana over time.

</Steps>

## Importing to TruthBounty

<Callout type="info">
  TruthBounty imports your Manifold trading history via their public API using your username.
</Callout>

### What Gets Imported

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Market** | Question/topic |
| **Position** | YES or NO |
| **Mana Invested** | Amount wagered |
| **Resolution** | Market outcome |
| **Profit/Loss** | Mana gained or lost |

### Connecting Your Account

1. Go to your TruthBounty dashboard
2. Select "Import from Manifold"
3. Enter your Manifold username
4. Authorize the connection
