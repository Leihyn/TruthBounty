## How Metaculus Works

<Steps>

### Create an account

Sign up for free on Metaculus.

### Explore questions

Browse open questions across various domains.

### Make predictions

Submit probability estimates (e.g., "30% chance by 2030").

### Update over time

Revise your forecasts as you learn new information.

### Build reputation

Earn points based on prediction accuracy (Brier score).

</Steps>

## Importing to TruthBounty

<Callout type="info">
  TruthBounty imports your Metaculus predictions via their public API using your username.
</Callout>

### What Gets Imported

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Question** | The forecasting question |
| **Your Prediction** | Your probability estimate |
| **Community Median** | What others predicted |
| **Resolution** | Actual outcome |
| **Brier Score** | Accuracy metric |

### Prediction Quality

Unlike binary prediction markets, Metaculus scores are based on:

1. **Calibration** - Do your 70% predictions happen 70% of the time?
2. **Resolution** - How close was your prediction to the outcome?
3. **Consistency** - Did you update appropriately with new info?
