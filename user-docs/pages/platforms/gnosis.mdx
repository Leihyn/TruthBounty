## How Omen Works

<Steps>

### Find a market

Browse active prediction markets on omen.eth.limo or other frontends.

### Buy outcome tokens

Purchase tokens representing your prediction (YES or NO).

### Trade or hold

You can trade your position anytime before resolution.

### Resolution

When the market resolves, winning tokens can be redeemed for xDAI.

</Steps>

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|

| **Amount** | xDAI invested |
| **Result** | Correct or incorrect |
| **Return** | Amount received on resolution |
