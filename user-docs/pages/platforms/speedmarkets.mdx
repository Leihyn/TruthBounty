## Understanding Win Rate

For SpeedMarkets:

```
Win Rate = (Correct Predictions / Total Predictions) √ó 100
```

<Callout>
  A 55%+ win rate is profitable long-term (depending on payouts). The best short-term traders hit 60-70%.
</Callout>

### Why Fast Predictions Are Hard

Short timeframes are essentially 50/50 unless you have an edge:
- Random noise dominates micro-movements
- Spreads and fees eat into edge
- Emotional decision-making is common

**The key:** Only trade when you see clear setups, not constantly.
