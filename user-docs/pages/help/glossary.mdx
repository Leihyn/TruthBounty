# Glossary

Definitions of key terms and concepts used in TruthBounty and the broader prediction market ecosystem.

## A

### AMM (Automated Market Maker)
A smart contract that provides liquidity for trading without requiring traditional order books. Prices are determined by mathematical formulas based on supply and demand.

### Azuro
A decentralized sports betting protocol that operates on multiple chains (Polygon, Gnosis). One of the platforms TruthBounty imports from.

## B

### Bankroll
Your total funds dedicated to prediction markets. Good bankroll management is essential for long-term success.

### BNB Chain
The blockchain where TruthBounty's core contracts are deployed. Formerly known as Binance Smart Chain (BSC).

### Bull/Bear
Common terms in crypto predictions:
- **Bull (Bullish):** Expecting price to go UP
- **Bear (Bearish):** Expecting price to go DOWN

## C

### Confirmation Bias
The tendency to seek information that supports your existing beliefs while ignoring contradictory evidence. A common psychological trap in predictions.

### Copy Trading
A feature that allows users to automatically replicate another trader's predictions. Followers pay fees to leaders when profitable.

### Cooldown
A waiting period between certain actions. For example, each platform has a 24-hour cooldown between imports.

## D

### dApp (Decentralized Application)
An application built on blockchain technology. TruthBounty is a dApp.

### Decimal Odds
A format for expressing betting odds. Example: 2.50 means winning $2.50 for every $1 bet (including your stake back).

### Diamond Tier
The highest tier in TruthBounty (TruthScore ≥ 5,000). Reserved for elite predictors.

### Drawdown
The decline from a peak to a trough in your balance. Managing drawdown is crucial for long-term success.

## E

### Edge
An advantage over other market participants. Having an edge means your expected returns are positive.

### Expected Value (EV)
The average outcome if a bet were repeated many times. Positive EV (+EV) means profitable long-term.

## F

### Follower
A user who deposits funds into a leader's copy trading vault to automatically replicate their trades.

## G

### Gas
Transaction fees paid to process blockchain operations. On BNB Chain, gas is paid in BNB.

### Gold Tier
A milestone tier in TruthBounty (TruthScore 1,000-1,999). Gold+ traders can become copy trading leaders.

## H

### Handicap (Spread)
A betting type where the favorite "gives" points to the underdog to even the odds. Example: Team A -3.5 means they must win by 4+ points.

## I

### Import
The process of bringing your prediction history from external platforms into TruthBounty.

### Implied Probability
The probability suggested by odds. Example: 2.00 odds implies 50% probability.

## K

### Kelly Criterion
A mathematical formula for optimal bet sizing based on your edge and bankroll. Often used at a fraction (e.g., "half Kelly") to reduce variance.

## L

### Leader
A Gold+ tier trader who has created a copy trading vault that others can follow.

### Leaderboard
The public ranking of all TruthBounty users by TruthScore.

### Limitless
A prediction market platform on Base chain that TruthBounty supports.

### Liquidity
The ease with which you can enter/exit positions. Low liquidity means harder to trade at desired prices.

## M

### MetaMask
A popular browser extension wallet for interacting with Ethereum-compatible blockchains.

### Moneyline
A simple bet on which team/outcome will win, without any point spread.

## N

### NFT (Non-Fungible Token)
A unique digital token on the blockchain. Your TruthBounty Reputation is represented as an NFT.

## O

### Oracle
A system that brings off-chain data (like sports scores or prices) onto the blockchain.

### Over/Under (Totals)
A bet on whether the combined score/result exceeds or falls below a specified number.

### Overtime Markets
A decentralized sports betting platform on Optimism that TruthBounty supports.

## P

### PancakeSwap Prediction
A crypto price prediction market on BNB Chain where users bet on whether BNB will go up or down in 5-minute rounds.

### Parlay (Accumulator)
A single bet that combines multiple predictions. All selections must win for the parlay to pay out.

### Platinum Tier
A high tier in TruthBounty (TruthScore 2,000-4,999).

### Polygon
A layer-2 scaling solution for Ethereum. Polymarket operates on Polygon.

### Polymarket
One of the largest prediction markets, focusing on news, politics, and current events on Polygon.

### Protocol Fee
The 10% fee TruthBounty takes from copy trading profits.

## R

### Reputation NFT
Your soulbound NFT that represents your identity and TruthScore on TruthBounty.

### Resolution
When a prediction market determines its outcome and pays out winners.

### ROI (Return on Investment)
Your profit or loss expressed as a percentage of your initial investment.

## S

### Simulation Mode
A feature allowing users to practice copy trading with virtual funds before committing real money.

### Slippage
The difference between expected and executed trade price, often due to market movement or low liquidity.

### Smart Contract
Self-executing code on a blockchain that automatically enforces agreements without intermediaries.

### Soulbound
A type of token that cannot be transferred. Your Reputation NFT is soulbound to your wallet.

### SpeedMarkets
A fast-paced crypto prediction platform supporting multiple assets and timeframes.

### Stake
The amount wagered on a prediction.

## T

### Tier
Reputation levels in TruthBounty: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond. Higher tiers unlock more features.

### TruthBounty
The decentralized reputation protocol you're learning about!

### TruthScore
The numerical representation of your prediction ability, calculated as Win Rate × √(Volume).

### TVL (Total Value Locked)
The total amount of funds deposited in a smart contract or vault.

## V

### Value Bet
A bet where you believe the true probability is higher than what the odds suggest, giving you an edge.

### Vault
A smart contract that holds funds for copy trading. Leaders create vaults; followers deposit into them.

### Volatility
The degree of price fluctuation. High volatility means bigger and more frequent price swings.

### Volume
The total amount traded. In TruthBounty, your volume contributes to your TruthScore.

## W

### Wallet
A software or hardware tool for storing cryptocurrency and interacting with blockchain applications.

### Win Rate
The percentage of predictions you got correct. A key component of your TruthScore.

### Wilson Score
A statistical method for calculating confidence intervals on proportions, used in TruthScore to handle small sample sizes fairly.

## Y

### Yield
The return generated by an investment, often expressed as a percentage.
