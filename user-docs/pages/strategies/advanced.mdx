## Risk Management at Scale

### Position Sizing Models

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-gray-50 dark:bg-gray-800 mt-4">
  **Kelly Criterion (simplified):**
  ```
  Optimal Bet % = (Edge / Odds) Ã— Confidence Factor
  ```

  **Conservative approach:** Bet 1/4 to 1/2 of Kelly suggestion to reduce variance.

  **Example:**
  - You think you have 55% edge on a 50/50 market
  - Full Kelly: 10% of bankroll
  - Half Kelly: 5% of bankroll (recommended)
</div>

### Drawdown Limits

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 mt-4">
  **Set hard limits:**

  | Drawdown Level | Action |
  |----------------|--------|
  | -10% from peak | Reduce position sizes |
  | -20% from peak | Stop trading, analyze |
  | -30% from peak | Extended break, full review |
  | -50% from peak | Consider if this is for you |
</div>

### Correlation Management

Don't concentrate risk:

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-blue-50 dark:bg-blue-900/20 mt-4">
  **Diversify across:**
  - Platforms (don't put everything on PancakeSwap)
  - Time (don't bet your month's budget in one day)
  - Types (mix crypto, events, sports)
  - Direction (not all bullish or bearish)
</div>
