## Tier FAQ

**Can I lose my tier?**
Currently, no. Your tier can only go up. Future updates may introduce tier maintenance requirements.

**How long does it take to reach Gold?**
It varies! Some active traders reach Gold in a few weeks, while others take months. Quality matters more than speed.

**Do higher tiers get more followers?**
Yes! Followers tend to prefer Gold+ traders. The verified badge builds trust.

**Is Diamond really achievable?**
Yes, but it requires exceptional performance over time. Think of it as a long-term goal rather than a quick achievement.
