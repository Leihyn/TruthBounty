## Viewing Your NFT

### On TruthBounty

Your NFT is displayed prominently on your Dashboard. You'll see the current image with your score and tier.

### In Your Wallet

#### MetaMask
1. Open MetaMask
2. Go to "NFTs" tab
3. Look for "TruthBounty Reputation"
4. Click to see details

#### Other Wallets
Most wallets that support NFTs will show your TruthBounty NFT. Look for it under "Collectibles" or "NFTs".

### On Block Explorers

You can view the raw NFT data on BscScan:
1. Go to [bscscan.com](https://bscscan.com)
2. Search for the contract: `0xa216b13c0b27b00c23552c768088fe11004d4b37`
3. Go to "Read Contract"
4. Call `tokenURI` with your token ID

## FAQ

**What happens if I lose access to my wallet?**
Unfortunately, if you lose your wallet, you lose your reputation. This is why it's crucial to keep your recovery phrase safe. There's no way to recover or transfer a soulbound NFT.

**Can I have multiple reputation NFTs?**
No, each wallet can only have one TruthBounty NFT. This is enforced by the smart contract.

**Does the NFT cost gas to update?**
No! The NFT metadata is generated dynamically on-chain. When your score changes, the NFT automatically reflects the new value without any transaction.

**Will my NFT work on other chains?**
Currently, the NFT exists only on BNB Chain. Cross-chain reputation is being explored for future updates.
