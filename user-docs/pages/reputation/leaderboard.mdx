# The Leaderboard

The TruthBounty leaderboard ranks all traders by their TruthScore. It's where you can see how you stack up against the best predictors in the world.

## How the Leaderboard Works

The leaderboard displays:
- **Rank**: Your position compared to all users
- **Address/Username**: Trader identity
- **TruthScore**: Their current score
- **Tier**: Their tier level
- **Win Rate**: Prediction accuracy
- **Platforms**: Which platforms they use

### What Affects Your Rank

Your rank is determined purely by TruthScore:
- Higher score = Higher rank
- Same score = Earlier registrant ranks higher (tiebreaker)

### Rank Changes

Your rank can change when:
- You import new predictions (score goes up)
- Other traders import (they might pass you)
- Predictions resolve (affects everyone's scores)

## Leaderboard Visibility

### Who Can See It

The leaderboard is public! Anyone can view it, even without connecting a wallet.

### Privacy

Your ranking shows:
- Wallet address (or username if set)
- TruthScore and tier
- Win rate and platforms
- General statistics

It does NOT show:
- Your individual trades
- Your exact balances
- Personal information

## Verification

Every ranking is verifiable:
- Scores are calculated from on-chain data
- Anyone can audit the calculations
- No fake accounts or inflated stats

## Leaderboard FAQ

**How often is the leaderboard updated?**
The leaderboard updates in near real-time as transactions are confirmed.

**Can I hide my ranking?**
No, leaderboard participation is automatic for all registered users.

**What if two people have the same score?**
The earlier registrant ranks higher (tiebreaker by registration date).

**Do I lose rank if I'm inactive?**
Currently, no. Your rank only changes based on score comparisons, not activity.
