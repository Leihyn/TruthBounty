## Quick Wins (Immediate Impact)

<Steps>

### Import All Platforms

If you've traded on multiple platforms, import them all!

- Each platform adds to your total volume
- More predictions improve your statistical confidence
- Diversification shows you're a well-rounded trader

### Wait Out the New Account Penalty

New accounts have a 50% score cap for 14 days. Just be patient:
- Day 1-14: Maximum 50% of calculated score
- Day 15+: Full score unlocked

### Reach 50 Predictions

Your score is scaled down until you have 50 bets:
- 10 bets = 20% of full score
- 25 bets = 50% of full score
- 50+ bets = 100% of full score

## Long-Term Strategies

### 1. Focus on Accuracy First

<div className="p-4 rounded-xl bg-green-50 dark:bg-green-900/20 mt-4">
  **The Math:**
  - 55% win rate + 100 BNB volume = Score ~735
  - 65% win rate + 50 BNB volume = Score ~690
  - 70% win rate + 30 BNB volume = Score ~613

  **Insight:** Accuracy matters more at lower volumes. At high volumes, they balance out.

- Don't bet on predictions you're unsure about
- Wait for high-conviction opportunities
- Keep track of what types of predictions you're best at

## Platform-Specific Tips

### PancakeSwap Prediction
- Watch BNB price trends before betting

- Look for mispriced markets

## Advanced: Score Optimization

For serious score hunters:

1. **Calculate your breakeven point**
   - At what win rate does your volume hurt you?
   - Focus on staying above that threshold

2. **Optimize import timing**
   - Import when you have batches of wins
   - The cooldown resets, so plan accordingly

3. **Cross-platform arbitrage**
   - Some platforms count wins differently
   - Understand how each platform affects your stats
