# Your TruthScore

Your TruthScore is the single most important number in TruthBounty. It represents your overall prediction ability and determines your tier, ranking, and access to features.

## What is TruthScore?

TruthScore is a number that combines:
- **How accurate you are** (win rate)
- **How much experience you have** (trading volume)

The formula is designed to be fair - you can't game it by just making tiny bets or by betting huge amounts with poor accuracy.

### Breaking It Down

**Win Rate Component:**
- If you win 60% of your predictions, your win rate is 60%
- This is the main driver of your score
- Accuracy matters most!

**Volume Component:**
- Uses the square root of your total volume
- This prevents whales from dominating purely by betting big
- Rewards consistent trading over time

### Example Calculations

| Trader | Wins | Total Bets | Win Rate | Volume | TruthScore |

**Key insight:** Carol has more volume than Dave, but Dave's higher accuracy gives them a similar score!

## Score Multipliers & Caps

### Minimum Requirements

<Callout type="warning">
  You need at least **10 predictions** to appear on the leaderboard.
</Callout>

This prevents people from showing up with 3/3 wins (100% accuracy) from lucky bets.

### Experience Scaling

If you have fewer than 50 bets, your score is scaled down:
- 10 bets = 20% of full score
- 25 bets = 50% of full score
- 50+ bets = 100% of full score

This rewards experienced traders while still giving newcomers a path.

### New Account Penalty

Accounts less than 14 days old have a 50% cap on their score. This prevents fake account manipulation.

## Anti-Gaming Measures

TruthBounty uses several techniques to keep scores fair:

### 1. Wilson Score Confidence

Instead of raw win rate, we use a statistical method that accounts for sample size:

| Scenario | Raw Win Rate | Actual Score Used |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| 3 wins / 3 bets | 100% | ~44% |
| 30 wins / 50 bets | 60% | ~47% |
| 600 wins / 1000 bets | 60% | ~57% |

This means lucky small samples don't beat consistent large samples.

### Practical Tips

1. **Focus on accuracy first** - A 70% win rate beats high volume with 50% accuracy
2. **Be consistent** - Regular trading builds score faster than sporadic big bets
3. **Diversify platforms** - Import from all platforms you use
4. **Give it time** - The 14-day cap lifts eventually, patience pays off

## Checking Your Score

Your TruthScore is displayed:
- On your Dashboard (prominently)
- On your NFT (dynamically updated)
- On your public profile
- On the leaderboard

It updates automatically when you:
- Import new predictions
- When pending predictions resolve
- When you manually refresh

## Score History

Your score can go up or down over time:
- **Increases:** When you import wins or have winning predictions resolve
- **Decreases:** Currently, scores only increase (future updates may add decay for inactive accounts)
