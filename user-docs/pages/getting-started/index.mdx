## Quick Start Steps

<Steps>

### Set Up Your Wallet

If you don't have a wallet yet, we recommend [MetaMask](https://metamask.io). It's free and takes 2 minutes to set up.

[Detailed wallet setup guide](/getting-started/wallet-setup)

### Connect to TruthBounty

1. Go to [TruthBounty](https://truthbounty.xyz)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top right
3. Select your wallet and approve the connection
4. Make sure you're on **BNB Chain** network

### Register Your Account

1. Click **"Register"** on the dashboard
2. Confirm the transaction (costs 0.0005 BNB)
3. Wait for your **Reputation NFT** to be minted
4. You're now a TruthBounty member!

[Detailed registration guide](/getting-started/registration)

### Import Your Predictions

1. Click **"Import Predictions"**
2. Select a platform (e.g., PancakeSwap)
3. Review your prediction history
4. Confirm the import
5. Watch your TruthScore update!

[Detailed import guide](/getting-started/first-import)

</Steps>

## What Happens Next?

After completing these steps:

| You'll Have | What It Means |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Reputation NFT** | A unique, soulbound token proving your identity |
| **TruthScore** | A number representing your prediction accuracy |
| **Tier Status** | Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, or Diamond |
| **Leaderboard Position** | Your global ranking among all traders |
