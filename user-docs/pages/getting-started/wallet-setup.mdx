# Setting Up Your Wallet

A crypto wallet is required to use TruthBounty. This guide will help you set one up if you don't have one already.

<Callout type="info" emoji="üí°">
  **Already have a wallet?** Skip to [Connecting Your Wallet](#connecting-to-truthbounty).
</Callout>

## What is a Crypto Wallet?

A crypto wallet is like a digital identity card that lets you:
- Sign in to Web3 apps (like TruthBounty)
- Hold cryptocurrency (like BNB)
- Approve transactions

**Your wallet doesn't actually store money** - it stores the keys that prove you own your assets on the blockchain.

## Choosing a Wallet

<Tabs items={['MetaMask (Recommended)', 'Trust Wallet', 'Other Wallets']}>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    ### MetaMask

    **Best for:** Desktop users, beginners

    MetaMask is the most popular crypto wallet and works great with TruthBounty.

    <Steps>

    ### Download MetaMask

    Go to [metamask.io](https://metamask.io/download/) and download for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge).

    ### Create a New Wallet

    1. Click "Create a new wallet"
    2. Create a strong password
    3. **IMPORTANT:** Write down your 12-word recovery phrase on paper
    4. Store it somewhere safe (not on your computer!)

    ### Add BNB Chain Network

    MetaMask doesn't have BNB Chain by default. Add it:

    1. Click the network dropdown (says "Ethereum Mainnet")
    2. Click "Add network"
    3. Click "Add a network manually"
    4. Enter these details:

    ```
    Network Name: BNB Smart Chain
    RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
    Chain ID: 56
    Symbol: BNB
    Block Explorer: https://bscscan.com
    ```

    5. Click "Save"

    </Steps>
  </Tabs.Tab>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    ### Trust Wallet

    **Best for:** Mobile users

    Trust Wallet is great if you primarily use your phone.

    <Steps>

    ### Download Trust Wallet

    Get it from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/trust-crypto-bitcoin-wallet/id1288339409) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wallet.crypto.trustapp).

    ### Create a Wallet

    1. Open the app and tap "Create a new wallet"
    2. Accept the terms
    3. **Write down your 12-word recovery phrase**
    4. Verify by selecting words in order

    ### Enable BNB Smart Chain

    Trust Wallet has BNB Chain built-in! Just:

    1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Wallets
    2. Make sure "Multi-Coin Wallet" is selected
    3. BNB Chain is automatically available

    </Steps>

## Getting BNB for Gas Fees

You'll need a small amount of BNB to pay for transactions on TruthBounty.

### Option 1: Buy BNB on an Exchange

1. Create an account on [Binance](https://binance.com), [Coinbase](https://coinbase.com), or another exchange
2. Buy some BNB (even $5-10 is enough to start)
3. Withdraw to your wallet address

### Option 2: Use Testnet (Free)

For testing, you can use the BNB Testnet with free test BNB:

1. Switch to "BNB Smart Chain Testnet" in your wallet
2. Go to the [BNB Faucet](https://testnet.bnbchain.org/faucet-smart)
3. Paste your wallet address
4. Click "Give me BNB"

<Callout type="warning" emoji="‚ö†Ô∏è">
  **Testnet vs Mainnet:** Testnet is for practice. For real trading and reputation building, use Mainnet.
</Callout>

## Connecting to TruthBounty

Once your wallet is set up:

<Steps>

### Go to TruthBounty

Open [truthbounty.xyz](https://truthbounty.xyz)

### Click Connect Wallet

Look for the button in the top right corner.

### Select Your Wallet

Choose MetaMask, WalletConnect, or another option.

### Approve the Connection

Your wallet will pop up asking for permission. Click "Connect".

### Check the Network

Make sure you're on "BNB Smart Chain" (for mainnet) or "BNB Testnet" (for testing).

</Steps>
