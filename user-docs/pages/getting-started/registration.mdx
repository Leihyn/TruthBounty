## Registration Steps

<Steps>

### Make Sure You're Connected

You should see your wallet address in the top right corner of TruthBounty. If not, [connect your wallet first](/getting-started/wallet-setup).

### Navigate to Dashboard

Click on **"Dashboard"** in the navigation menu.

### Click Register

You'll see a **"Register"** button if you haven't registered yet. Click it.

### Confirm the Transaction

Your wallet will pop up asking you to confirm:
- **To:** TruthBounty Core Contract
- **Amount:** 0.0005 BNB
- **Gas Fee:** ~0.0001 BNB (varies)

Click **"Confirm"** in your wallet.

### Wait for Confirmation

The transaction takes about 3-15 seconds to confirm. You'll see:
- A loading spinner
- Then a success message
- Your new NFT displayed!

## What Happens On-Chain

When you register, the following happens on the blockchain:

1. **Profile Created:** Your wallet address is registered in the TruthBounty smart contract
2. **NFT Minted:** A unique ERC-721 token is minted to your wallet
3. **Score Initialized:** Your TruthScore starts at 0
4. **Timestamp Recorded:** Your registration date is saved (affects some bonuses)

## Troubleshooting

### "Transaction Failed"

<Callout type="error">
  **Common causes:**
  - Not enough BNB for gas fees
  - Wrong network selected
  - Network congestion (try again)
</Callout>

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you have at least 0.001 BNB in your wallet
2. Verify you're on BNB Smart Chain (not Ethereum)
3. Wait a minute and try again

### "Already Registered"

If you see this message, you already have an account! Go to your Dashboard to see your profile.

### "Connection Lost"

If your wallet disconnects during registration:
1. Refresh the page
2. Reconnect your wallet
3. Check the Dashboard - your registration may have completed
